## Challenge

BII Magazine sought to extend their established print publication into the digital realm while preserving the editorial quality and visual sophistication readers expected.

## Solution

We developed a digital reading experience that honors print design traditions while leveraging digital capabilities. Interactive elements enhance storytelling without overwhelming the content.

## Results

The digital edition successfully expanded the magazine's reach while maintaining the premium feel of the print publication.
