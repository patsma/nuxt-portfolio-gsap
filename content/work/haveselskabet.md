## Challenge

Haveselskabet needed a mobile app that could serve their community with gardening guidance, seasonal advice, and member benefits in an accessible format.

## Solution

We designed an intuitive app experience with personalized gardening calendars, plant guides, and community features. The UI emphasizes visual plant identification and seasonal relevance.

## Results

The app increased member engagement and provided a valuable new touchpoint for gardening enthusiasts.
