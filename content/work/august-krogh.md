## Challenge

The project required creating a digital platform that would honor August Krogh's scientific legacy while making his contributions accessible and engaging for contemporary audiences.

## Solution

We developed an immersive experience combining archival materials with modern interactive design. The visual direction respects historical context while feeling contemporary and accessible.

## Results

The platform successfully introduces new audiences to August Krogh's contributions to science through an engaging digital experience.
