## Challenge

ScienceNews required a digital platform that could present scientific news in an engaging way while maintaining credibility and readability.

## Solution

We designed a clean, modern interface with strong typographic hierarchy and strategic use of interactive elements. The design balances visual engagement with reading comfort.

## Results

The redesigned platform increased reader engagement and time-on-site while improving content accessibility.
