## Challenge

SuperBrugsen sought to enhance the in-store shopping experience with digital touchpoints that would provide value to customers while reinforcing their brand.

## Solution

We designed interactive in-store experiences including digital signage, product information displays, and wayfinding solutions. The design language maintains brand consistency while serving practical in-store needs.

## Results

The digital touchpoints improved customer experience metrics and provided valuable interaction data for store optimization.
