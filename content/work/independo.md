## Challenge

Independo needed a mobile app that would be accessible and empowering for their user base, with particular attention to usability and inclusive design.

## Solution

We developed an app with strong accessibility foundations, clear visual hierarchy, and intuitive interactions. The art direction creates a warm, supportive atmosphere.

## Results

The app successfully serves its users with high accessibility scores and positive user feedback on ease of use.
