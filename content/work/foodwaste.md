## Challenge

FoodWaste needed a digital platform that could effectively communicate the urgency of food waste issues while providing practical tools for reduction.

## Solution

We designed an approachable, action-oriented interface that combines impactful statistics with practical guidance. The visual direction makes the serious topic accessible without diminishing its importance.

## Results

The platform increased awareness and user action around food waste reduction, supporting the organization's mission.
