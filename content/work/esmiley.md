## Challenge

eSmiley's existing platform needed modernization to better serve food industry professionals managing compliance and safety documentation.

## Solution

We redesigned the platform with a focus on efficiency and clarity. The new interface streamlines workflows while introducing a more contemporary visual language.

## Results

The redesign improved user satisfaction and task completion rates, making compliance management more efficient for users.
