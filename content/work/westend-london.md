## Challenge

Westend London needed a digital platform that could capture the glamour and excitement of London's theatre district while serving practical visitor needs.

## Solution

We developed an elegant design that balances aspirational imagery with functional event discovery and booking flows. The art direction evokes theatrical sophistication.

## Results

The platform successfully drives theatre bookings while establishing a strong digital presence for the Westend brand.
